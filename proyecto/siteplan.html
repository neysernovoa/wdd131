Discover Lambayeque

Site Name

Discover Lambayeque

The site name “Discover Lambayeque” was chosen because it clearly represents the goal of the website, which is to invite visitors to explore and learn about the Lambayeque region. The word “Discover” makes the site feel welcoming and attractive to tourists and travelers.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Discover Lambayeque website is to provide information about tourist attractions, cultural places, and important locations in the Lambayeque region. The site helps visitors understand what they can visit and why Lambayeque is an important destination in Peru.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Deep Space Blue (#1d2d44). Used for the site header, navigation and headings.
  • Secondary Color: Eggshell (#F0EDB8). Used for the page background and section backgrounds.
  • Accent Color: Dusty Denim (#748cab). Used for links, highlights, and accents.
  • Text Color: In black (#0d1321). Used for body text and paragraphs to ensure readability and good contrast.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Playfair Display, used for headings.
  • Body Font: Roboto, used to body text, paragraphs, list
